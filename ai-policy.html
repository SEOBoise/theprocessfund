Introduction

The Process Fund (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to the responsible and ethical use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies in delivering educational content and services. This AI Policy outlines how we develop, deploy, and manage AI-driven features on theprocessfund.com.

Purpose of AI Use

We leverage AI to enhance user experience, including personalized content recommendations, automated customer support, and data analytics to improve site performance and content relevance.

Responsible AI Principles

  • Transparency: We disclose when content or interactions are AI-generated or AI-assisted.
  • Fairness: We strive to minimize bias in datasets and model outputs.
  • Accountability: All AI systems are overseen by qualified staff who review performance and make improvements.
  • Privacy: We respect user privacy and process data in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
  • Security: We implement safeguards to protect data used in AI models from unauthorized access.

Data Collection and Usage

We collect only the data necessary to power AI features, such as anonymized usage statistics and user preferences. Personal data is processed according to strict security and retention policies and is never sold to third parties.

User Control

Users may opt out of certain AI-driven functionalities where feasible by adjusting their preferences or contacting us. Essential AI functions that maintain site security or basic operations cannot be disabled.

Continuous Improvement

We regularly audit AI models for accuracy, fairness, and relevance. Feedback from users and internal assessments inform iterative improvements.

Third-Party AI Services

When we integrate third-party AI tools, we ensure they comply with our standards and do not compromise user data or trust.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about our AI practices, please contact us at info@Theprocessfund.com.
